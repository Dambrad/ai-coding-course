## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Success

### Weekly Habits
- **Code daily**: Even 15-20 minutes helps
- **Take notes**: Create your own `notes/` folder
- **Build projects**: Apply concepts to YOUR interests
- **Ask questions**: Use Claude/ChatGPT when stuck
- **Join community**: Discord, Reddit, GitHub

### Learning Techniques
- **Type, don't copy**: Manual typing builds muscle memory
- **Break things**: Experiment and see what happens
- **Read errors**: They tell you exactly what's wrong
- **Debug systematically**: print() is your friend
- **Version control**: Commit early, commit often

### Avoiding Pitfalls
- **Don't skip basics**: Modules 0-1 are essential
- **Don't just watch**: Code along with videos
- **Don't isolate**: Community helps tremendously
- **Don't rush**: Understanding > Speed
- **Don't compare**: Everyone learns at their pace
